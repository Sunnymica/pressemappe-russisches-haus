⚖️ The unlawful arrest – 18 December 2024 +
  • Henry Lindemeier was arrested and handcuffed in front of the Russian House and taken to a communal cell
  • Four hours in custody, followed by forensic identification procedures
  • Alleged offences: insults and breach of the peace in previous weeks – neither substantiated
  • Police sent a written apology – and declared the matter closed
  • In subsequent legal proceedings, the police formally acknowledged: the deployment was unlawfult-online, May 2025
  • The day before, Lindemeier had called the police – because he had been threatened: "You'll get a knife in the back one day." The police did not comet-online, May 2025

  • Men accompanying her physically shoved Lindemeier away
  • When he called loudly for police, officers told him to leave – not the aggressors
