📄 German-Russian Agreement 2011 +
4 February 2011

Bilateral Cultural Agreement Germany–Russia

Governs the status of the Russian House in Berlin and the Goethe-Institut in Moscow. Obliges Germany to pay the property tax: €70,000 annually from the federal budget.

→ Unilateral termination only possible after 99 years – at the earliest in 2110. The Foreign Office cites Article 26 of the Vienna Convention.
🇪🇺 EU Sanctions against Rossotrudnichestvo +
July 2022

EU places Rossotrudnichestvo on sanctions list

Legal consequence according to the Foreign Office: "Freezing of assets and a prohibition on making funds available." No commercial events, no revenues, no economic activity.

→ The Russian House continues to operate language courses, a restaurant, concerts – potentially in violation of the sanctions.
⚖️ Ruling: Bavarian Administrative Court – Electricity bill +
24 June 2025

Bavarian Administrative Court of Appeal – Bundesbank v. Russian House

The Russian House sued over the Bundesbank's refusal to pay electricity bills. The bank had refused on grounds of suspected sanctions violations.

→ Court ruled in favour of the Bundesbank. How the house has covered its operating costs since then – unknown, no response to enquiries.
⚖️ Unlawful arrest of Lindemeier +
18 December 2024

Declaratory judgment proceedings – Berlin Police

Henry Lindemeier was arrested in front of the Russian House, handcuffed and subjected to identity processing. Allegations: insult and breach of the peace – neither substantiated. The police formally acknowledged: the operation was unlawful.

→ Confirmed by court: the arrest had no legal basis.
🔍 Ongoing proceedings +
Ongoing since 2024

Berlin Public Prosecutor – Foreign Trade and Payments Act

Investigation into suspected undisclosed subletting of apartments with concealed rental income. Doorbells show numbers only.

→ Proceedings ongoing. Outcome pending.
Ongoing since 2024

Berlin Data Protection Commissioner – Surveillance cameras

Examination of whether cameras illegally filmed public streets. This footage was used as evidence against Henry Lindemeier.

→ Examination ongoing. Outcome pending.
December 2025 / January 2026

European Commission (FISMA) – Complaint regarding EU law violations

Complaint submitted regarding possible violations of EU sanctions law through the continued operation of the Russian House. Case number: CPLT(2026)00148. The Commission is examining the matter on the basis of applicable EU law and will report on findings and any measures taken.

→ Complaint registered. Examination ongoing. Possible consequence: infringement proceedings against Germany.
