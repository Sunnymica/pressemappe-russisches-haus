📄 German-Russian Cultural Agreement 2011 +
4 February 2011

Bilateral Cultural Agreement – Germany and Russia

Governs the status of the Russian House in Berlin and the Goethe-Institut in Moscow. Commits Germany to paying the property tax for the Berlin complex: €70,000 per year from the federal budget.

→ Unilateral termination is only possible after 99 years – at the earliest in 2110. The Foreign Office invokes Article 26 of the Vienna Convention on the Law of Treaties.
🇪🇺 EU Sanctions on Rossotrudnitschestwo +
July 2022

EU places Rossotrudnitschestwo on sanctions list

Legal consequence according to the German Foreign Office: "Freezing of assets and a prohibition on making funds available." This means: no commercial events, no revenue, no economic activity.

→ The Russian House continues to run language courses, a restaurant, concerts and theatre – potentially in violation of EU sanctions.
⚖️ Ruling: Bavarian Administrative Court – Electricity Bills +
24 June 2025

Bavarian Administrative Court – Bundesbank v. Russian House

The Russian House challenged the Bundesbank's refusal to settle electricity bills. The bank had refused because the high energy costs indicated commercial activity and therefore a possible sanctions violation.

→ The court ruled in favour of the Bundesbank. How the Russian House has covered its operating costs since then is unknown – enquiries go unanswered.
⚖️ Unlawful Arrest of Lindemeier +
18 December 2024

Continuation proceedings – Berlin Police

Henry Lindemeier was arrested in front of the Russian House, handcuffed, and subjected to forensic identification procedures. The alleged offences – insult and breach of the peace – were unsubstantiated. In subsequent legal proceedings, the police formally acknowledged that the deployment was unlawful.

→ Confirmed by court: the arrest had no legal basis.
